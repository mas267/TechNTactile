About Tech N' Tactile

Welcome to the Tech N' Tactile Podcast, where we explore the intersection of technology, gaming, and accessibility. Our mission is to provide insights and discussions that empower our listeners to engage with the latest trends in the industry.

Our Mission

Tech N' Tactile is the podcast where brothers unite to explore the fascinating intersection of technology, video games, and accessibility! Join us as we dive into the latest trends in tech and gaming, while shining a spotlight on the vital importance of making these worlds inclusive for everyone, regardless of ability.

Each episode, we share our insights, experiences, and discussions on everything from cutting-edge gadgets to the most exciting game releases. But we don’t stop there! We’re passionate about advocating for accessibility in tech and gaming, highlighting innovative solutions and tools that empower individuals with disabilities.

Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a gamer, or someone interested in how technology can create a more inclusive world, Tech N' Tactile is your go-to source for engaging conversations and practical advice. Tune in as we celebrate the power of technology to enhance lives and break down barriers, one episode at a time!

Meet the Hosts

Our hosts are passionate about technology and accessibility, bringing their unique perspectives to each episode.

Cody was born visually impaired and shares a passion for exploring the intersection of gaming, technology, and inclusivity. Cody’s perspective as a visually impaired gamer provides valuable insights into the challenges and opportunities in making games and tech more accessible. His firsthand experiences with screen readers, adaptive gaming setups, and inclusive design bring a unique voice to the podcast, advocating for a world where technology is accessible to all.

Michael, Cody’s brother, is a tech enthusiast and an Integrated Information Technology major at the University of South Carolina. Born with cerebral palsy, Michael lacks the use of the majority of his limbs and fine motor control. Despite these challenges, he has developed a profound appreciation for assistive technologies and adaptive tools that enable independence and creativity. Michael’s insights into accessibility come from personal experience, making his contributions to the podcast both authentic and inspiring.

Together, Cody and Michael bring a dynamic and heartfelt approach to Tech N' Tactile. Their bond as brothers and their shared mission to promote accessibility make each episode a meaningful exploration of how technology can break down barriers and create opportunities for everyone.
